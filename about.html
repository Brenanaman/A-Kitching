ABOUT

He is well known for his expressive use of metal and wood letterforms while producing limited edition prints and visuals for commissions. In 1964, Kitching was influenced by Anthony Froshaug at Watford College of Technology, where he cofounded the Experimental Printing Workshop. He began letterpress printing at Omnific Studios after becoming a partner there.

Alan Kitching was chosen a member of Alliance Graphique International (AGI) and named Royal Designer for Industry (RDI) in 1994. Alan is a visiting professor at the University of the Arts London and an honorary fellow of the Royal College of Art. In the UK, Europe, Australia, and South Africa, Alan has presented at design conventions, art schools, and industry events.
